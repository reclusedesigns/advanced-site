Our Process

Getting started

We meet to figure out the scope of your project. Whether it be a multi-unit development or your dream home, we spend time with you to undertstand your exact needs helping us tailor an accurate quote.

The paperwork

We write up a contract for you outlining milestones, timelines and the cost of the job. Building permits are acquired and when you're ready to start, we're ready to build.

Leave things in our hands

Once we start building, we're always within reach, so feel free to be as involved in this process as you like.
